ABOUT THE GAME

Vinnie the wolf was on a space exploration when a worm hole appeared thrusting him to the unknown reaches of space. Vinnie must find his way back to earth dodging many scary obstacles and extraterrestrial beings. HELP HIM!

Lost in Space is a deep, immersive game of survival and perseverance, with unexpected outcomes. Vinnie is relying on YOU. You would have a chance to go on a journey through several solar systems to get to the Vinnie’s final destination. But don’t expect this journey to be smooth: you would have to find a way to the right wormhole and get along with aliens that will be trying to attack outlander.

"We were working hard on creating fun, yet unexpected game for our target users. We hope you will enjoy it"

Galactic Wolves Team
